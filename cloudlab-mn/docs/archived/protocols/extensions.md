**Status:** CONCEPTUAL

## 1. Fractal Agency (The "Daughters" Protocol)

- **Concept:** Emily is not a Monolith. She is a **Bicameral Organism**.
- **Mechanism:**
  - When a complex task arises (e.g., "Audit Crypto Wallets"), Emily performs **Cognitive Mitosis**.
  - She spins up a temporary **Cognition Process** (SGLang Instance).
  - She injects specific **Context** (Finance, Auditing) into the instance.
  - The Process lives for the duration of the task, reports back to the Architect, and then **Terminates**.
- **Impact:** Massive parallel processing without context pollution. The "Instinct" remains pure; "Cognition" handles the heavy lifting.
